Introduction
Taking a loan is a big financial decision, whether it is for buying a home, car, or funding personal needs. Before applying for any loan, it is important to understand how much you will pay every month and how much interest you will be charged.

Our Loan & EMI Calculator helps you calculate your monthly EMI (Equated Monthly Installment), total interest, and total repayment amount instantly. This allows you to plan your finances better and choose the right loan. Instead of doing complicated manual calculations, this tool provides quick and accurate results in seconds.

What is EMI?
EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay your loan. EMI includes Principal amount (loan amount) and Interest amount. Each EMI payment reduces your loan balance over time.

Why Use a Loan EMI Calculator?
Calculating EMI manually is difficult and prone to errors. Using this calculator helps you get instant results, plan your monthly budget, compare different loan options, avoid financial surprises, and understand total interest cost.

🧮 EMI Formula

The EMI is calculated using the following formula:

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1]

Where:

  • • P = Loan Amount
  • • R = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate ÷ 12 ÷ 100)
  • • N = Number of Months
