🔢 Word Counter – Quick Reference

What's Being Counted?

  • Words: Groups of characters separated by spaces
  • Characters: All visible characters and spaces
  • No Spaces: Only visible characters (no spaces)
  • Sentences: Text ending with . ! or ? followed by a space
  • Paragraphs: Blocks of text separated by blank lines

📝➡️🔢 Words to Number – Quick Reference

How It Works

This tool converts written English numbers into their numeric form. Simply type the number in words and get the numeric value instantly.

Examples

Input: eighty-eight thousand eight hundred eighty-eight

Output: 88,888

Input: one million two hundred thirty-four thousand

Output: 1,234,000

Input: negative forty-two

Output: -42

Supported Features

  • Negative Numbers: Use "negative" or "minus" prefix
  • Flexible Input: Works with hyphens or spaces
  • Word "and": Automatically ignored
