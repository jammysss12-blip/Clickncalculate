How to Calculate Your Exact Age

Finding your exact age is simple with our Age Calculator:

  1. Enter Your Date of Birth — Select your birth date using the date picker (day, month, year).
  2. Select the Target Date — By default this is today's date, but you can change it to any date you want.
  3. Click Calculate — Instantly see your exact age in years, months, and days.

Age Formula:

Age = Target Date − Date of Birth

Example: If you were born on March 15, 2000, and today is May 30, 2026 — your exact age is 26 years, 2 months, and 15 days.

The age calculator is useful for filling out official forms, checking eligibility for programs or events, calculating retirement dates, and tracking birthdays and anniversaries.

How to Convert Height Between Feet, Inches & Centimeters

Height conversion is needed for medical forms, fitness tracking, visa applications, and more. Here is how to use our Height Calculator:

  1. Choose Your Input Unit — Select whether you want to enter your height in feet/inches or centimeters.
  2. Enter Your Height — Type your height value (for example, 5 feet 9 inches, or 175 cm).
  3. Click Convert — Instantly see your height in the other unit.

Height Conversion Formulas:

  • Centimeters to Feet: cm ÷ 30.48 = feet
  • Feet to Centimeters: feet × 30.48 = cm
  • Inches to Centimeters: inches × 2.54 = cm
  • Centimeters to Inches: cm ÷ 2.54 = inches

Example: 5 feet 9 inches = 175.26 cm
