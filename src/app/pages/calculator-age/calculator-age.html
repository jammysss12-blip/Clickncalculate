🎂 1. Age Calculator

What Does It Do?
The Age Calculator determines your exact age based on your date of birth.

How to Use

  1. Enter your Date of Birth
  2. Select the current date (or leave default as today)
  3. Click Calculate

What You Get

  • • Age in years, months, days
  • • Total age in days, hours, minutes, seconds
  • • Time until next birthday

Age Calculation Formula
Age = Current Date – Birth Date

📏 2. Height Calculator

What Does It Do?
The Height Calculator converts and calculates height between different units.

Supported Units

  • • Feet & Inches
  • • Centimeters (cm)
  • • Meters (m)

Conversion Formulas

  • • Feet to Centimeters: Height (cm) = Feet × 30.48
  • • Inches to Centimeters: Height (cm) = Inches × 2.54
  • • Centimeters to Feet: Height (feet) = cm ÷ 30.48

⏰ 3. Time Calculator

What Does It Do?
The Time Calculator helps you find the difference between two times or dates.

Features

  • • Calculate time between two dates
  • • Add or subtract time
  • • Convert time units

How to Use

  1. Enter Start Date & Time
  2. Enter End Date & Time
  3. Click Calculate

Time Calculation Formula
Time Difference = End Time – Start Time

🔄 4. Time Unit Converter

What Does It Do?
Converts time between different units.

Supported Conversions

  • • Seconds ↔ Minutes
  • • Minutes ↔ Hours
  • • Hours ↔ Days
  • • Days ↔ Weeks
  • • Years ↔ Months

Common Formulas

  • • 1 Minute = 60 Seconds
  • • 1 Hour = 60 Minutes
  • • 1 Day = 24 Hours
  • • 1 Year ≈ 365 Days
