What Does It Do?
The Percentage Calculator calculates the percentage based on obtained marks and total marks.

Formula
Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100

How to Use

  1. Enter Obtained Marks
  2. Enter Total Marks
  3. Click Calculate

Example
• Obtained Marks: 420
• Total Marks: 500
• Percentage = (420 ÷ 500) × 100 = 84%

📝 4. Required Marks Calculator

What Does It Do?
This feature helps you calculate how many marks you need to achieve a target percentage or grade.

Formula
Required Marks = (Target % × Total Marks ÷ 100)

  • • Required Marks: (85 × 500 ÷ 100) = 425

How is Percentage Calculated?

Calculating a percentage is simple using this formula:

Percentage = (Marks Obtained ÷ Total Marks) × 100

Example: If you scored 78 out of 100, your percentage = (78 ÷ 100) × 100 = 78%

Example: If you scored 450 out of 600, your percentage = (450 ÷ 600) × 100 = 75%
