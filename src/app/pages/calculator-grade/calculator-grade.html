🧮 1. Percentage Calculator

What Does It Do?
The Percentage Calculator calculates the percentage based on obtained marks and total marks.

Formula
Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100

How to Use

  1. Enter Obtained Marks
  2. Enter Total Marks
  3. Click Calculate

Example
• Obtained Marks: 420
• Total Marks: 500
• Percentage = (420 ÷ 500) × 100 = 84%

🎯 3. GPA Calculator

What Does It Do?
The GPA Calculator calculates your Grade Point Average based on subject grades and credit hours.

Standard GPA Scale

  • • A+: 4.0
  • • A: 4.0
  • • B: 3.0
  • • C: 2.0
  • • D: 1.0
  • • F: 0.0

Formula
GPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credit Hours

📝 4. Required Marks Calculator

What Does It Do?
This feature helps you calculate how many marks you need to achieve a target percentage or grade.

Formula
Required Marks = (Target % × Total Marks ÷ 100)

Example

  • • Target Percentage: 85%
  • • Total Marks: 500
  • • Required Marks: (85 × 500 ÷ 100) = 425

Why It's Useful
Exam preparation, goal setting, performance improvement
